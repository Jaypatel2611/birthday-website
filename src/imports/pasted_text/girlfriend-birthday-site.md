## SECTION 3 — PREMIUM MEMORY GALLERY

---

### GALLERY REQUIREMENTS

The number of photos/videos should be dynamic.

The design must support:

* 10 photos
* 21 photos
* 50 photos
* Any future number

Do NOT hardcode image counts.

Instead create reusable gallery cards.

Each gallery card must contain:

* Photo or video placeholder
* Memory title placeholder
* Date placeholder

Example:

Photo

Title:
"Forever And Always"

Date:
14 February 2025

---

### HOVER EFFECT (MANDATORY)

When hovering over any gallery card:

Image slightly zooms.

Dark overlay fades in.

Memory title appears.

Date appears.

Example:

Forever And Always ❤️

14 February 2025

Animation:

* Smooth scale
* Overlay fade
* Text slide upward
* Premium transition timing

Must feel similar to luxury portfolio websites.

No abrupt animations.

---

### MOBILE BEHAVIOR

Since mobile devices do not have hover:

When user taps a gallery card:

Overlay should appear.

Second tap opens enlarged image/video.

---

### GALLERY CARD STYLES

Allow different card sizes.

Examples:

* Small portrait card
* Large portrait card
* Square card
* Landscape card
* Video card

The gallery should automatically arrange them beautifully.

---

### DATE DISPLAY

Every memory card must reserve space for a date.

Example formats:

20 June 2025

14 February 2025

01 January 2025

The date should be part of the hover overlay.

---

### VIDEO SUPPORT

Some cards may contain videos instead of images.

Requirements:

* Play icon overlay
